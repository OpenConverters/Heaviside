## Books & Dissertations

- **"A New Small-Signal Model for Current-Mode Control"** -- Ridley PhD dissertation (VPI, 1990). The definitive current-mode control model. Free PDF: CurrentModeControl.pdf (225 pages)
- **"Power Supply Design Vol. 1"** -- Book excerpts available: BookExcerpts.pdf. Covers nine topologies, modeling, voltage-mode compensation, current-mode control, current-mode compensation, and frequency response measurement.

## Seminal Papers Available

- **Middlebrook 1976** -- Original input filter interaction paper (Middlebrook1976BritishLibrary.pdf)
- **Kelkar Dissertation** -- Input filter analysis (KelkarFilterPartI.pdf)
- **Dowell's Equations for Multiwinding Transformers** -- Chapters 1-9 in two PDFs

- **POWER 4-5-6** -- Earlier design software. Manual: POWER_4-5-6_Manual.pdf
