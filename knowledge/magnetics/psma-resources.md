# PSMA Resources for Power Magnetics
Source: Power Sources Manufacturers Association (www.psma.com)
Contact: power@psma.com

## About PSMA
The Power Sources Manufacturers Association (PSMA) is a multinational power electronics association. The PSMA Magnetics Committee is a group of volunteers knowledgeable about power magnetics, with members from power supply companies, wound components producers, core manufacturers, consultants, and academia. The committee meets monthly by webconference and is open to anyone interested.
